What makes Birdsvew different

Built for learning,
not engagement.

Verified STEM Only

Every lesson passes an AI quality gate before rendering. Pseudoscience is rejected at the pipeline level — not moderated after the fact.

Manim-Rendered Visuals

Not slides or stock footage. Mathematically precise animations — the same toolkit behind 3Blue1Brown, generated for your exact question.

No Watch-Time Algorithm

No autoplay rabbit holes, no engagement bait. Lessons end. You close the app. That's the point.

Infinite Topics On Demand

From quantum field theory to Bayesian inference — if it's STEM, Birdsvew generates a visual lesson for it. Your curriculum, your rules.

Private or Monetised

Keep lessons private, bundle them into paid playlists, or publish structured courses. What you generate is entirely yours.

Expressive AI Narration

Studio-quality AI voice synthesis — expressive, human-paced, frame-accurate to the animation. No robotic TTS.
